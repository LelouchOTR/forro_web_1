Forró Pé de Serra

Experience the traditional roots of Forró with the accordion-led Pé de Serra style. This intimate, rhythmic dance connects directly to the rural traditions of Northeast Brazil.

Weekend Intensives

Our Dance Universe

Discover the different dimensions of Forró culture and community

Forró Universitário

Popular among younger dancers, this more acrobatic style developed in urban university settings with influences from other dance forms. Expect dynamic movements and creative variations.

Live Music Events

Experience the authentic energy of Forró with our quarterly live music events. From traditional trios featuring accordion, zabumba and triangle to modern fusions with local bands.
